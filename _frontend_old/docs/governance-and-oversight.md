# Governance and Oversight

## Human Approval
- All curriculum updates require Ministry approval.
- AI-generated content must comply with approved frameworks.

## What is Required
- Lessons must reference Liberia MOE standards.
- Offline alternatives must exist for all activities.
- Teacher instructions provided for every activity.
- Mixed literacy and shared-device scenarios considered.

## What is Prohibited
- High-bandwidth content without offline alternatives.
- Lessons requiring physical lab equipment without substitutions.
- Unauthorized copyrighted materials.
- Bypassing human review or approval processes.

## Data Ownership & Security
- Student data privacy maintained at all times.
- Role-based access enforced for dashboards.
- Audit logs kept for all AI-assisted outputs.

## Certification Boundaries
- No claims of formal certification outside approved pathways.
- Pathways to IT certifications (CompTIA, AWS, etc.) noted for optional enrichment.
