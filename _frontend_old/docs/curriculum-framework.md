# LiberiaLearn Curriculum Framework

## Cognitive Bands & Pedagogy
- **Foundation (Grades 1–3):** Concrete, visual, play-based learning.
- **Core (Grades 4–9):** Abstract thinking, problem-solving, early STEM rigor.
- **Specialization (Grades 10–12):** Career readiness, WAEC alignment, advanced labs.

## Subject Areas
- Mathematics
- English Language
- Science
- Social Studies
- Digital Literacy
- Life & Civic Education

## Assessment Philosophy
- Formative assessments for ongoing feedback.
- Summative assessments for mastery.
- Multiple attempts allowed for formative work.
- Immediate feedback for self-paced learning.
- Teacher oversight available for intervention.

## Labs & Practical Learning
- Parameter-based and scenario-driven virtual labs.
- Offline-capable simulations.
- Incrementally upgradable to richer WebGL/Unity experiences.
